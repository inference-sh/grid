## Key Notes

- Credit unit base price: **$0.14/unit** (prepaid packs)
- Failed tasks are **not charged**
- V3.0 / Video-O1 / Video-3O bill **per second** (3-15s range)
- V2.x and older bill **per clip** (5s or 10s)
- Audio generation roughly doubles the video cost
- 4K mode is ~3.75x the cost of pro mode
- V2.5 Turbo is the most cost-effective at $0.21/5s clip
